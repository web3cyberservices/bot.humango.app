
HUMANGO BOT AUDIT SCOPE v1.2
============================

HumangoBot performes a comprehensive semantic and technical audit of web resources.
The following checks are active as of 2025:

1. CONTENT DISCOVERY
- Deep crawl of all accessible internal links.
- Identification of legal sections: Privacy, Cookies, Terms, Impressum, Contact.
- Multi-language keyword matching (EN, DE, FR, IT, ES, PL).

2. LEGAL DOCUMENT ANALYSIS
- Completeness Audit: Verification of mandatory GDPR clauses (Art. 13/14).
- Data Categories: Identification of IP, Cookies, PII collection disclosures.
- User Rights: Verification of mentions regarding Erasure (Art. 17), Portability (Art. 20), Access (Art. 15).
- Retention Periods: Detection of data storage and deletion schedules.
- Recency Check: Identification of "Last Updated" dates; flagging documents older than 12 months.

3. LANGUAGE & TRANSPARENCY
- Local Language Matching: Ensuring legal documents are available in the website's primary interface language.
- Semantic Clarity: Identification of vague or non-compliant processing purposes.

4. TECHNICAL COMPLIANCE
- HTTPS Enforcement: Verification of SSL/TLS encryption for all pages.
- Status Monitoring: Ensuring all legal documents return 200 OK.
- Link Integrity: Automated detection of broken internal links within legal texts.
- Cookie Consent: Detection of Consent Management Platforms (CMP) and tracking scripts.

5. SECURITY HEADERS
- Verification of CSP (Content Security Policy).
- HSTS (HTTP Strict Transport Security) header presence.
- X-Frame-Options (Clickjacking protection).
